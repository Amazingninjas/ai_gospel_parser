## ğŸ¥ Production Tips

### Recording Setup
- **Screen Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Browser Zoom:** 110-125% for readability
- **Recording Software:** OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Cursor:** Enable cursor highlighting in OBS
- **Audio:** Clear voiceover (Blue Yeti or similar)

### Visual Tips
- **Mouse movements:** Slow and deliberate
- **Typing:** Medium speed (not too fast)
- **Pauses:** 1-2 seconds after each action
- **Highlighting:** Use cursor movement to guide viewer's eye

### Editing
- **Background Music:** Soft, non-distracting (optional)
- **Transitions:** Simple fades between scenes
- **Text Overlays:** Highlight key features
- **Speed:** 1.1x-1.2x for efficiency (optional)

### Export Settings
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080
- **Frame Rate:** 30 or 60 fps
- **Bitrate:** 8-12 Mbps
