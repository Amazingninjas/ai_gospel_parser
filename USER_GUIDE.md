## Verse Lookup

### How to Search for Verses

1. Click in the search box
2. Enter a verse reference in one of these formats:
   - **Book Chapter:Verse** - `John 3:16`
   - **Book Abbreviation** - `Jn 3:16`
   - **With "1" or "2"** - `1 John 1:9`

3. Click "Search" or press Enter

### Supported Books

All 27 New Testament books are supported:
- Matthew, Mark, Luke, John
- Acts, Romans, 1 Corinthians, 2 Corinthians
- Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians
- 1 Thessalonians, 2 Thessalonians, 1 Timothy, 2 Timothy
- Titus, Philemon, Hebrews, James
- 1 Peter, 2 Peter, 1 John, 2 John, 3 John
- Jude, Revelation

### Understanding the Verse Display

When you search for a verse, you'll see:

1. **Greek Text (SBLGNT):** The original Greek text
2. **English Text (WEB):** English translation for reference
3. **Clickable Words:** Each Greek word is clickable

**Click any Greek word to:**
- See its lexicon entry instantly
- View Strong's number
- Read definition and usage
- (Mobile) Auto-switch to Lexicon tab

## Lexicon Panel

The Lexicon Panel shows detailed information about Greek words.

### How to Access Lexicon Entries

**Method 1: Click Words in Verse Display**
- Click any Greek word in the verse
- Lexicon entry appears automatically

**Method 2: Search by Strong's Number**
- Click Strong's number next to a word
- View complete lexicon entry

### Lexicon Information Includes

1. **Greek Word:** Original Greek spelling
2. **Transliteration:** How to pronounce it
3. **Pronunciation:** Phonetic guide
4. **Strong's Number:** Reference number (e.g., G25)
5. **Definition:** Primary meaning
6. **Usage:** How the word is used
7. **Derivation:** Word origin
8. **Thayer's Lexicon:** Detailed scholarly definition
9. **Moulton-Milligan:** Papyri examples
10. **Morphology Stats:** Tenses, cases, occurrences
11. **See Also:** Related words

### Morphology Statistics

For verbs and nouns, you'll see:
- **Total Occurrences:** How many times it appears in the NT
- **Tenses:** Different tenses found (present, aorist, etc.)
- **Cases:** Grammatical cases (nominative, accusative, etc.)

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem:** Can't log in
**Solutions:**
- Verify your email and password are correct
- Check for typos
- Try registering if you haven't created an account
- Clear your browser cache and try again

**Problem:** "Session expired" message
**Solution:** Your session has expired after 7 days. Log in again.

### Verse Lookup Issues

**Problem:** "Verse not found"
**Solutions:**
- Check the verse reference format: `Book Chapter:Verse`
- Verify the book name is spelled correctly
- Try using a book abbreviation: `Jn` instead of `John`
- Ensure the verse exists (e.g., there's no John 100:1)

**Problem:** Greek text not displaying properly
**Solutions:**
- Your browser supports Greek automatically
- Try refreshing the page
- Update your browser to the latest version

### AI Chat Issues

**Problem:** AI not responding
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Reload the page and try again
- The AI service may be temporarily unavailable

**Problem:** AI response is slow
**Solutions:**
- Be patient - streaming responses take time
- Complex questions take longer to answer
- Check your internet speed

### Lexicon Issues

**Problem:** "No lexicon entry found"
**Solutions:**
- The word may not have a Strong's number
- Try clicking a different form of the word
- Some particles and conjunctions have limited data

### General Issues

**Problem:** Page not loading
**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl/Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Problem:** Mobile layout looks wrong
**Solutions:**
- Rotate your device to portrait mode
- Zoom out if zoomed in too far
- Clear browser cache
